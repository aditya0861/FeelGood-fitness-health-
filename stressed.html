Stressed Workout Plan

Release stress and boost your mood with these effective exercises!

1. Walking

A simple yet effective way to boost circulation and wake up the body.

Pros: Low impact, improves cardiovascular health, easy to do anywhere.

Cons: May be monotonous, weather-dependent.

2. Swimming

Swimming provides a full-body workout that relaxes the mind and body.

Pros: Low-impact, full-body workout, calms the mind.

Cons: Requires a pool, may not be accessible everywhere.

3. Pilates

Pilates strengthens the core and improves flexibility, making it a great stress reliever.

Pros: Builds core strength, improves posture, low impact.

Cons: Requires equipment, can be challenging for beginners.
