Happy Mood Workout

Boost your mood and stay fit with these exercises!

1. Yoga

Focuses on breathing and mindfulness while improving flexibility.

Pros: Reduces stress, improves flexibility, enhances mood.

Cons: Requires practice to master poses, may not provide intense cardio.

2. Running

A classic cardiovascular exercise that releases endorphins.

Pros: Boosts heart health, easy to start, excellent stress reliever.

Cons: Can be hard on joints, requires good running shoes.

3. Strength Training

Builds strength and improves overall fitness.

Pros: Builds muscle, improves posture, boosts metabolism.

Cons: Requires equipment, may feel intimidating for beginners.

4. Cycling

A low-impact workout that's great for your legs and heart.

Pros: Easy on joints, great for endurance, enjoyable outdoors.

Cons: Needs a bike, weather-dependent if outdoors.
